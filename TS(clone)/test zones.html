TEST ZONES

Note:

(1) HYDERABAD is divided into FOUR ZONES to facilitate the candidates to take the TS EAMCET-2023 in nearby area and without any difficulty.

(2) During the ONLINE submission of TS EAMCET-2023 Application form, the candidate is hereby informed to select the required ZONES of his / her choice and once chosen, request for change of ZONE will not be entertained later.

(3) The Convener reserves the right to add or delete some Test Centres from the list of Zones notified.

(4) The Convener reserves the right to allot the candidates to any Zone other than that opted by the candidates.

(5) Candidate has to submit only one application either for ‘E’ or ‘AM’ or ‘E & AM’ category test. If any candidate submits more than one application for one category, the Convener reserves the right to reject all the applications or accept any one of them only.
